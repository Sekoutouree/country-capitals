West African Coutries: Their Capitals and Currencies

West Africa, a region with total population above 360 million, has 16 countries. It occupies about 8 million sq km of the continent of Africa, with most countries being part of Sub Saharan Africa. The region is endowed with many natural resources.

The region is experiencing tremendous growth in socio-economic development and is the among the continent's rapidly evolving regions.
